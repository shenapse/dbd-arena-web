The shared global rule for the 1v1 Symmetric format. It applies to every killer.
Per-killer detail (maps, win conditions, perk and add-on limits) lives in 
the sibling per-killer balancing pages, which complement this rule.

## Killer ping

The killer must hold a stable connection below 60 ms for the trial to
count.
Persistent high ping or instability is handled as a remake.

## Starting the chase timer

<Aside type="note" title="Summary">
  The round follows the common "crouch and response" starting convention at Killer Shack.
</Aside>

Each round must begin at Killer Shack. The survivor chooses one of the
shack's two doorways and takes position there. The killer must take
position at the opposite doorway.

Once both players are in position, the survivor gives the start signal
by performing one quick crouch. Upon seeing the signal, the killer must
immediately perform the designated start response.

The designated start response—such as missing a basic attack or using
the killer's power—is specified in the applicable per-killer rule.

The chase timer starts when the survivor performs the start signal. If
the killer does not perform the designated response, or performs it
noticeably late, the players must reset and repeat the starting
procedure.

The survivor has the right to choose which doorway they use before the
round begins.

## Surrender

The survivor may surrender at any time.

The killer may surrender once achieving their applicable win condition
is no longer possible.

A surrender causes the surrendering player to lose the current round.
It does not forfeit the entire match. A killer surrenders by
disconnecting from the trial.
