A **regulation** is one named, published set of match rules — the format you play
under. When you join the queue you queue for one regulation, and you are only
matched with other players who chose the same one.

{/* Each regulation has a **status** that says whether you can play it right now.
Upcoming and archived regulations are not queueable.

- <StatusBadge status="active" />: Playable now — queueable through Discord.
- <StatusBadge status="upcoming" />: Announced but not yet open.
- <StatusBadge status="archived" />: No longer playable. Kept as historical record. */}
