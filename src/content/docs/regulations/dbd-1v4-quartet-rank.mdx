| Aspect | Value |
|---|---|
| Team structure | 1 × Killer vs 4 × Survivors |

| Voice comms | Yes — all four survivors are assumed to be in voice with one another. |

## Queue requirement

Survivors are expected to queue as a full four-player party.

| Who | How they queue | Declares at queue time |
|---|---|---|
| Killer player | Queues solo. | One killer character. |
| Survivor party | Queues as a group of four. | Nothing. |

## Result facts

| Fact | Notes |
|---|---|
| Winning side | Which side won — killers or survivors. Omitted on a draw. |
| Draw | Marks a drawn match. Mutually exclusive with a winning side. |
| Generators remaining | How many generators were left unrepaired at the end (0-5). |
| Hooks | Total hooks performed by the killer during the match (0-12). |
| Kills | How many survivors were killed (0-4). |

## Ratings

Strict per-killer rating within this regulation. A match affects

- for Killer player: his rating for the killer character
- for Survivor party: the **survivor group** rating for the killer character they face.

e.g., suppose player K playing Nurse vs survivor team S=(s1, s2, s3, s4).
affected ratings are:
- (K, \{1v4 Quartet Rank regulation / role=killer / killer=Nurse\})
- (S, \{1v4 Quartet Rank regulation / role=survivor / killer=Nurse\})
- **NOT** (**s1**, \{1v4 Quartet Rank regulation / role=survivor / killer=Nurse\})
- **NOT** (S, \{**some other  regulation** / role=survivor / killer=Nurse\})
- **NOT** (S, \{some other  regulation / role=survivor / killer=**Blight**\})

See the [Ratings](/handbook/ratings/) guide for how ratings work.
