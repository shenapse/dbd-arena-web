Some killers require extra constraints — or relaxed ones — to keep matches
competitive. These per-killer adjustments are published here as addenda to the
regulation's general rules. A killer not listed here plays under the base
regulation with no additional changes.

Read the [general rule](/regulations/1v4-quartet/rules/general/) first; these
entries only cover what differs per killer.
