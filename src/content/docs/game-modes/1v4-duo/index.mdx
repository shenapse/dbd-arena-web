### Survivor perk restriction scope

In Duo+Duo Ranked, the standard scope for survivor-side perk restrictions is
each duo separately. A restriction listed on a per-killer balancing page
therefore applies to both duos, but each duo is checked only against its own
two survivors, not against all four survivors.

A per-killer balancing page may define a finer scope, such as a per-survivor restriction,
but it never defines a coarser team-level restriction across both duos.
This means each duo can validate its build without consulting the other duo.

The same rule applies to item restrictions.
See **Perk Restriction Types** above for more detailed explanation.

## Queue requirement

Survivors are expected to queue as a Duo.

| Who | How they queue | Declares at queue time |
|---|---|---|
| Killer player | Queues solo. | One killer character. |
| Survivor player | Queues as a group of four. | Nothing. |

## Result facts

| Fact | Notes |
|---|---|
| Winning side | Which side won — killers or survivors. Omitted on a draw. |
| Draw | Marks a drawn match. Mutually exclusive with a winning side. |
| Generators remaining | How many generators were left unrepaired at the end (0-5). |
| Hooks | Total hooks performed by the killer during the match (0-12). |
| Kills | How many survivors were killed (0-4). |
