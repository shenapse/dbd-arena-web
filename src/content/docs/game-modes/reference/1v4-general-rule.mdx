The shared general rule for 1v4 game modes (Quartet, Duo+Duo, and future 1v4
formats). It applies to every killer across those modes. Per-mode and
per-killer restriction detail (maps, win conditions, perk and add-on limits)
lives in each game mode's own balancing pages, which complement this rule.

## Killer ping

The killer must hold a stable connection below 60 ms for the trial to
count.
Persistent high ping or instability is handled as a remake.

## Survivor character choice

Each survivor must choose a different survivor character. Duplicate survivor
characters are not allowed within the same trial.

- Banned character:
  - Ada Wong

## Required offerings

- Killer: Cut Coin
- Survivor:
  - Shroud of Union — Causes all Survivors to spawn together in the same location.
  - one of Hatch control offerings:
    - in a map with shack: Annotated Blueprint — Make it spawn by the killer shack
    - in a map without shack: Vigo's Blueprint

## In-match restrictions

### Self-unhook

The AFC (Anti Face-Camp) system must be turned off in the custom match settings.

Survivors must not attempt to self-unhook.
The only exception: Deliverance perk.

### Hook sabotage

Survivors must not complete a hook sabotage action.
This also applies when survivors are allowed to bring a toolbox or a perk that enables sabotage.

### Chests

Survivors must not complete the open chest action.

## Result counting and surrender

### Hook and kill count

The hook count increases each time a survivor reaches a new hook stage.
A dead survivor always counts as 3 hooks, no matter how that survivor died.

If a survivor escapes through the hatch before all generators are completed,
that survivor is counted as killed.

### Surrender result

A side surrenders by disconnecting from the match. When a side surrenders,
the match stats are finalized at the time of the disconnect. The surrendering
side is treated as doing nothing after that point.

Examples:

- If the killer surrenders at 5 hooks, 0 kills, and 2 generators remaining,
  the final result is 5 hooks, 0 kills, and 0 generators remaining.
- If the survivors surrender at 5 hooks, 0 kills, and 2 generators remaining,
  the final result is 12 hooks, 4 kills, and 2 generators remaining.

### When surrender is allowed

- Either side may surrender once that side can no longer achieve its win
condition.
- Survivors may also surrender when all survivors are downed and unable to act.
In that case, one survivor disconnect is enough to surrender for the survivor
side. If only one survivor is still standing, only that survivor may surrender for
the survivor side.
- You **MAY NOT** DC mid-game even if you have already achieved the win-con. You should play it out unless the opponent wants to move on.
